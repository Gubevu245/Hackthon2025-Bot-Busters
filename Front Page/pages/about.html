Our Story

The Nazareth Tertiary Students Association (NaTeSA) is a non-profit organization for tertiary students who are members of the Nazareth Baptist Church EBuhleni. Founded in 2000, NaTeSA was created by merging several university-based student societies such as:

  • MaNaSO - Mangosuthu University of Technology
  • NBCSS - University of Zululand
  • MSNSS - Durban University of Technology

Our Mission

We believe in education as a powerful weapon against poverty, following the teachings of Prophet Isaiah Shembe. Our goals include:

  • Promoting academic excellence and leadership
  • Providing career guidance and development
  • Conducting workshops for community development
  • Becoming the leading academic association in Southern Africa

Leadership Structure

NEC (National Executive Committee)

Oversees all South African universities, manages policies, and has the highest access level.

BEC (Branch Executive Committee)

Leads individual university branches, manages activities, and reports to NEC.

General Members

Students registered under a specific branch who participate in campus activities.

Our Impact

Over the years, NaTeSA has supported students through:

  • Career guidance and talent development
  • University applications and registration assistance
  • Leadership development programs
  • Community service initiatives
